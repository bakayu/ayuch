Hey all!

Recently I got the opportunity to take on the role of technical lead at [Open Source Chandigarh](https://www.linkedin.com/company/open-source-chandigarh), and it's been an exciting journey so far. My friend Rehan (Who is also our mentor at Open Source Chandigarh) and I have been discussing an idea for a while now: conducting a workshop on Linux at our campus.

We wanted to show people that Linux isn't just an Operating System, but a viable alternative to windows and macOS, and the superior choice for developers and power users alike.

*(We are two big nerds who talk about nerdy things all the time so this was supposed to be our biggest chance to nerd it out in front of many people.)*

After some brainstorming with the team, the idea on was finalized as conducting a workshop on the Linux Command-Line.

As part of this workshop, I decided to write a **tutorial on the linux cli -- in the form of a blog series** which anyone can go through to learn basic linux cli tools.:

**[Read the linux-cli tutorial](/blog/linux-tutorial/)**

This series will take you from terminal newcomer to confident command-line user. I Hope your next "Open the terminal" encounter is more enjoyable :P
